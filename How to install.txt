Hi there!

1-Make sure you install the Better Discord: https://betterdiscord.net/home/ 

2-After installing Better Discord, go to your settings>Themes>Open theme folder

3-Drag the ´D3sk1ng.theme.css` on theme directory

4-After this, go to your discord and press CTRL+R, this will restart your discord

5-Now go to Settings>Themes>and enable the option from the D3sk1ng Theme!

6-Enjoy !
